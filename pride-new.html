A running list of the pride flags that my shop offers jewelry for. Use the sidebar to quickly navigate to specific flags.

The most recently added flags are berrisexual, almondsexual, genderflux, and gaybian.

Not every flag is available in all jewelry styles. Feel free to shoot me a message on Etsy if you'd like to request any missing flags in any specific style and I'll add them to my listings if I have the bead colors.
